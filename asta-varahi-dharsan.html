Meet: Varahi Dasan

Devotion, Service, and Spiritual Leadership

Varahi Dasan, a humble and devout servant of Jai Varahi Amman, is the visionary behind the establishment of this sacred temple. With an unwavering faith in the Goddess and a deep understanding of her divine essence, Varahi Dasan dedicated his life to spreading her teachings and guiding others on their spiritual path.

Inspired by divine visions and his own transformative experiences, Varahi Dasan undertook the monumental task of establishing a temple that would serve as a spiritual sanctuary for all devotees seeking solace and divine intervention. His leadership and tireless service have been instrumental in creating a community centered around devotion, faith, and spiritual growth.

OUR IMPACTS

Our event aimed to create lasting positive change in our community. Below we share what we planned and what we achieved through the collective effort of our volunteers, supporters, and participants.

What We Planned

  • Plant 500 trees across the community
  • Reach 1,000 visitors to our event
  • Provide food supplies to 200 families
  • Host 25 local vendor stalls
  • Engage 50 local volunteers

What We Achieved

  • Planted and distributed 1000+ trees
  • Welcomed 5000+ visitors to our event
  • Provided food supplies to 750+ families
  • Filled all 50 stall bookings with local vendors
  • Engaged 100+ dedicated volunteers
