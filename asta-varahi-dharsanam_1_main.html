About Asta Varahi Dharishanam

Asta Varahi Dharishanam is a grand spiritual celebration that brings together devotees from across the world to experience the divine presence of Sri Kottai Varahi Amman. This two-day event features powerful rituals, spiritual discourses, and cultural programs.
