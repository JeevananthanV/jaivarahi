About Asta Varahi 2.0

Asta Varahi 2.0 is a grand spiritual celebration that brings together devotees from across the world to experience the divine presence of Sri Kottai Varahi Amman. This three-day event features powerful rituals, spiritual discourses, and cultural programs that uplift the soul and bring prosperity to all participants.

The Story of the Event

The Inception

Founded to revive ancient Tamil spiritual culture, the Asta Varahi event was created to spread Sri Varahi Amman worship through traditional Tamil rituals and Siddhar-inspired practices. Rooted in devotion and heritage, this sacred movement preserves Tamil culture, strengthens spiritual consciousness, and reconnects devotees with timeless divine traditions.

Evolution to 2.0

Entering a new phase of growth, the Asta Varahi event expands its sacred mission to revive ancient Tamil spiritual culture and spread Sri Varahi Amman worship beyond boundaries. Guided by traditional Tamil rituals and Siddhar wisdom, this journey strengthens Tamil heritage, deepens devotion, and carries timeless spiritual practices to future generations.
