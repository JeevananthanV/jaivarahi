At Kottai Varahi Temple, we offer spiritual services to support the well-being of devotees. We perform Pooja for peace, protection, and positive energy. Our Astrology (Jothidam) guidance helps people understand life issues and find traditional remedies. Powerful Homas are done to remove obstacles and bring prosperity. We also conduct Parihara Poojas and special rituals for those facing planetary or personal challenges. Our Ashta Varahi Prayers invoke the eight divine forms of Varahi Amman for strength, success, and blessings. All activities are performed with devotion to help devotees receive the grace of Varahi Amman.
