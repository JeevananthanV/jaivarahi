Kamadhenu Pooja Procedure (Vidhanam)

Kamadhenu Pooja is dedicated to the Holy Cow Kamadhenu, the mother of all cows on earth. She is equated with Mother Earth as she nourishes and sustains all living beings. Kamadhenu is also known as Surabhi and Nandini. The word ‚ÄúKam‚Äù in her name signifies her power to fulfill the wishes of her devotees. All gods and goddesses in the Hindu pantheon reside within Kamadhenu.

Kamadhenu Pooja Mantra 
Om Sarvadevamaye Devi Lokanam Shubandini
Matru Mama Bhishitam Safalam Kuru Nandini.

Benefits of Worshipping Kamadhenu (Goddess Gomatha)

  • Wealth: Bestows abundant prosperity and financial stability.
  • Happiness: Grants peace, harmony, and joy in life.
  • Prosperity: Blesses the devotee with success and growth in all aspects of life.

How to Perform Kamadhenu Pooja

  • The puja should be performed in the afternoon, facing north.
  • Light a lamp with ghee mixed with turmeric.
  • Offer fragrant dhoop and apply tilak using kesar.
  • Use marigold (ghenda) flowers for worship.
  • Prepare food using besan (gram flour) on this day.
  • Chant the given mantra 108 times using a sandalwood mala.
  • Feed the cow with fresh grass, jaggery, and bananas.

Special Offerings

  • Offer urad dal ki kachori (black gram delicacy) to a black cow for sin redemption.
  • Feed fresh green grains and grass to a cow for wish fulfillment.
  • Offer jaggery to a black cow for relief from difficulties.
